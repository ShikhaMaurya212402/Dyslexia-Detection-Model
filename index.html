Features

Our AI-Powered Dyslexia Detection Tool offers the following key features:

  • Handwriting Analysis: Detects dyslexia signs by analyzing handwriting samples using advanced AI.
  • Voice and Speech Analysis: Assesses reading and speaking patterns to identify difficulties.
  • Reading Pattern Recognition: Monitors eye movement and reading speed for early signs.
  • Instant Results: Get quick, reliable feedback with personalized recommendations.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Easy to use for parents, teachers, and professionals.
  • Data Privacy: We ensure all user data is encrypted and never shared.

How It Works

Our dyslexia detection tool operates in three simple steps:

  1. Input Collection: Users provide handwriting samples, voice recordings, and reading exercises.
  2. AI Analysis: Advanced AI algorithms analyze the inputs to detect patterns indicative of dyslexia.
  3. Results & Guidance: The system delivers instant results with personalized recommendations to support learning.
